Welcome to Tombstone Arizona!

  • Tombstone is a small mining town founded in 1877 in deep southern Arizona. This town resonates with the Old West atmosphere! You can visit nearly every single historical attraction they have to offer.

  • The town was built due to the discovery of a large amount of silver in the San Pedro river. Their local silver mines produced $0 - $85 million worth of silver buillion. Click Here to learn more about the silver mine.

  • During the time the area was being prospected, the Chiricahua Apache Indians were the natives in southern Arizona. They had a long term battle with the US Military and prospectors coming to the area. Click Here to learn about the Chiricahua Apache Indians.

  • The town is largely famous for the gunfight at Ok Corral. The shootout mostly settled a long running fued between the Earp and the Clanton families. There is even a reenactment show that you can watch!
